Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Anand Motor Product

Q: How does Anand Motor ensure consistent product quality?

We maintain ISO 9001:2015 certification and operate a state-of-the-art facility with an 11-tank phosphating plant combined with mechanical treatment. Our fully computerized manufacturing process ensures every product meets stringent quality standards. Each component undergoes rigorous testing before dispatch.

Q: What is the extent of Anand Motor's product range?

We offer over 2,500+ products including rubber parts, filters (300+ varieties), lubricants, bearings, ignition locks, spark plugs, gaskets, and complete engine parts. This extensive range makes us the widest product supplier in India, allowing dealers to source everything from a single manufacturer.

Q: How quickly can Anand Motor fulfill orders?

A: We guarantee rapid dispatch within 24 hours, ensuring your supply chain remains efficient and your inventory is well-managed. Our streamlined logistics and 32,000 sq. mtrs manufacturing facility enable us to process orders quickly without compromising quality.

Q: How does Anand Motor support its distributors?

We provide comprehensive support including free freight on orders over ₹25,000, exclusive area rights for 3 years, dedicated sales team assistance, marketing materials, technical training, and a loyalty app for retailers and mechanics. Our 1000+ employee team is committed to your success.

Q: What kind of ROI can distributors expect with Anand Motor?

Our distributors enjoy better margins by partnering directly with the manufacturer, eliminating middlemen costs. With high-demand products, strong brand recognition since 1978, and no strict sales targets, dealers typically see improved profitability within the first year. Our extensive product range also reduces inventory complexity and increases turnover.

Q: How does Anand Motor help distributors manage their inventory?

We offer flexible ordering with no mandatory bulk purchases of ₹2-4 lakh that strain finances. Our wide product range from a single supplier simplifies inventory management. With 24-hour dispatch and 5000+ pan-India retail points, you can maintain optimal stock levels without tying up excessive capital.

Q: What are Anand Motor's global capabilities?

We have established brand presence in 15+ countries with proven international market penetration. Our products meet global quality standards, and we provide export support to distributors looking to expand beyond India. This global expertise gives our partners confidence in product quality and reliability.

Q: How does Anand Motor stay current with industry regulations?

With 46 years of experience since 1978, we maintain continuous compliance with industry standards through ISO 9001:2015 certification, regular audits, and investment in modern technology. Our R&D team stays updated with latest automotive regulations and emission norms, ensuring all products meet current and upcoming standards.
